SOCIAL AND COMMUNITY FEATURES

"A sense of community can enhance user engagement. The proposed solution incorporates social features to encourage interaction:"

  • Social sharing and collaborative lists: Users can share their watchlists and collaborate on group recommendations.
  • Community reviews and ratings: A verified review system to maintain authenticity and trust in user-generated content.
  • Friend activity tracking: Users can follow friends to see what they are watching, rated, or recommended.
